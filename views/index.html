NCEHR is a Nigerian based inter-disciplinary and inter university collaboration that monitors the link between health and climate change through vaious indicators that highlights the threats to health, and proffering solutions with community members as co-partners, co-learners, and co-owners of knowledge produced from the group.

Learn More

  • Goals

    Equitable and Sustainable Development of All.

  • Vision

    Advancing equitable ecosocial knowledge and praxis approaches within the context of community-academic partnership.

  • Mission

    To advance and leverage on community-academic research approach and agency of all partners for the co-creation, production, and ownership of knowledge for evidenced-based effective praxis to improve societal health and change.

  • Values

    Equity, Inclusion among Diversities, Peace, Integrity/transparency, Humility, Capacity Building, Ecosocial health, Sustainability, Scholarship.
